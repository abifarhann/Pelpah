About.

We are a company providing export standard banana leaves that is committed to maintaining product quality and quality. We utilize the abundant potential of Indonesian banana leaves and empower the potential of Indonesian banana farmers.

Vision.

Pelpah has a vision to export Indonesian natural products worldwide. We build trusted brands, expand market reach, increase product value, empower local farmers, and preserve nature. Together, we can bring Indonesia's natural wealth to the global stage and provide benefits to all stakeholders.

Mission.

Pelpah has a mission to improve the quality of Indonesia's natural products. We improve production quality, strengthen supply chains, increase product innovation and diversification, build brands and promotions, and strengthen cooperation. Together, we can take Indonesia's natural products to a higher level and increase their competitiveness in the global market.

Product.

Grade A banana leaves from king bananas and plantains. Fresh and high-quality, perfect for food wrapping, and decoration.

Grade B.
Grade B banana leaves sourced from fresh, healthy, and quality Indonesian bananas. With a leaf length of 80-100 cm.
